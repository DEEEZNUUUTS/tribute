Kobe Bryant

Listed below is Kobe's timeline

  • 1978- August 1978 Kobe Bryant was born in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, U.S.A

  • 1996- At the age of 17 years old, Kobe Bryant was selected 13th pick by the Charlotte Hornets and was eventually traded to the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 2000- Kobe Bryant win his first championship with the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 2001- Kobe Bryant won back to back championship with the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 2002- Kobe Bryant and the Los Angeles Lakers were able to won their third championship.

  • 2008- Kobe Bryant won his first MVP award (Most Valuable Player).

  • 2009- Kobe Bryant won his fourth ring as a member of the Los Angles Lakers. He also won his Finals MVP.

  • 2010- Kobe Bryant won his fifth and Last Championship with the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 2016- Kobe Bryant retired after 20 NBA seasons with the Los Angeles Lakers.
